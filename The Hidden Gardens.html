Hidden Gardens is an add-on to the Tramway arts centre on the Southside, it is a peaceful garden that organises versatile events. The greenspace was founded in 2003 by NVA, a company/registered charity specialised in organising environmentally innovative contemporary art practice.

The Hidden Gardens is Scotland's first sanctuary garden and it is dedicated to peace. NCA website, in fact, states that Hidden Gardens is a "permanent reminder that Glasgow and much of the UK were deeply opposed to the Iraq war" (Source)

There are many ways to get involved, Hidden Garden organises family-friendly events, cooking classes, offers volunteering opportunities and many workshops.
