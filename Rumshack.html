Venture out to the Rum Shack in Shawlands for some delicious cocktails and Caribbean food with music and rum selection of over 100 varieties! The bar/venue holds a nice-sized crowd and they are often hired out by Glasgow acts. In addition, they often facilitate touring musical acts and music events from all over the UK.

The Rum Shack, as the name suggests, promotes rum culture but also stocks up on international beers from places such as Mexico, Cuba, Trinidad and Brazil and offers original cocktails with a latin American twist.
