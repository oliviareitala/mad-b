For Fika Sake is a cafe based in Partick, only established in 2016, and inspired by Swedish food. (1) Fika is characterised by a warm, cosy and friendly atmosphere, with comfortable seats and couches for people to have an enjoyable time and pleasant time.

In Fika one can drink coffee, eat sandwiches or have a more artsy experience. The cafe hosts, in fact, several workshops, such as pottery, life drawing, music nights played by local musicians and yoga. Moreover, in the cafe there are art works on display too. In Scandinavia, the meaning of FIKA sums up all of this: “FIKA is togetherness, FIKA is community and FIKA is a good cup of coffee and indulgence in a treat or two”. (2)

G5G recommends:
Pottery workshops (Saturdays, 11am - 2pm - a workshop for beginners and non-beginners, each person can create three pieces learning how to make them only with their hands and also with the potters wheel.)
Life drawing sessions (Tuesday and Thursdays, 6pm.)
