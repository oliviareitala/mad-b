Civic House is a venue for events, research and projects, and home to the urban development platform Agile City. The site plays host to grass-roots community projects that exhibit the best of local arts, innovations and activities, offering summer school opportunities and (1).

The research conducted and exhibited by the Civic House covers an enormous multi-disciplinary array of subjects, including architecture, education, finance, food, regeneration, tech and digital, and urbanism. In its quest towards social innovation and community projects, the facilities act as a collaborative hub for the North Glasgow Canal area (2).

The venue has worked in conjunction with the Pipe Factory and the Glue Factory, two similar platforms for local art and innovation (3).
