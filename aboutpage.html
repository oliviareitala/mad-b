Gimme Five, Glasgow consists of four dedicated digital media students at University of Glasgow: Olivia, Silvia, Adam and Laura. What sparked this project was our mutual interest towards the art scene in Glasgow as well as the central Glasgow districts that all have exciting new things emerging. We all agreed that even though social media and online magazines are full of tips and "What's On"-type of content on this city, there was a real lack of websites dedicated to more curated content.

What followed was drafting the concept of Gimme Five, Glasgow. We wanted to reach out to all people in Glasgow who are passionate about communities and independent art. The purpose of this web page is to show that exciting, new things happen all over in Glasgow and not only the 'obvious' locations and massive institutions. Just because not all venues can afford massive advertising budgets, it doesn't mean they don't have compelling art to exhibit.

We want to encourage our users to really explore their city, beyond the lines of their own immediate area. We are firm believers that great things happen through exploring the culture happening around us every day and Gimme Five, Glasgow is here to enable these urban adventures.

Enjoy and explore!
Olivia, Silvia, Adam and Laura
