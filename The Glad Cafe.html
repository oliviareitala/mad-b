Glad Cafe is a little yet very active hub just off Queen's Park with regular events for all budgets and interests. Glad Cafe states on their website that it "has something to offer people of any age or background in this diverse and vibrant local community." (Source)

The Glad Cafe has two associated companies, Glad Rags Thrift Shop (non-profit cooperative business for eco-friendly shopping) and the Glad Foundation (Scottish Charitable Incorporated Organisation to promote community arts). With regular events (music, film, talks, storytelling, exhibitions etc.), The Glad Cafe aims to add to the "representation of southside creativity" (Source) which is why it is well on our list of recommendations.

The Glad Foundation is a sister company of The Glad Cafe and its aim is to support the cafe's community purpose. It started with small community arts initiatives such as samba drumming with young carers and regular music groups for young people with autism. Today, the foundation receives support from Creative Scotland and continues to organise various creative workshops and cultural events for people from all walks of life.
